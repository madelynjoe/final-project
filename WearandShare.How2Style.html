STYLE TIPS

A Pop of Color!







A pop of color can always brighten your outfit. When you don’t want to think too much about what to wear, go with a basic look and just wear a colorful item to make the outfit! It is so perfect to complete a simple outfit and make you stand out in effortless way.








Oversized Clothing







When wearing an oversized sweater or pant, try to pair it with fitted clothing. You want to stay away from drowning your frame.Pairing with something baggy would make you look shapeless, so go toward straight-fitted jeans or a fitted top.








Bold with Solid








When wearing a bold or busy clothing piece, combine the pattern with something solid. You do not want the patterns to clash, but rather compliment each other.









Attitude is Key








No matter what you are wearing, your attitude can show a sense of confidence that no piece of clothing can match. A confident attitude is the ultimate fashion accessory.
