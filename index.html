WHY FASHION?

Fashion is important because it is a great way of self-expression. Clothes and fashion play largely into who you are. It allows you to express yourself and how you are feeling without saying anything. It makes a statement and it can boost your self-esteem. Clothing is an amazing tool that allows us to express who we are as individuals. The better you dress, the better you feel!

WHAT IS WEAR & SHARE?

Wear & Share is a website made by 3 Girls Who Code who are interested in using computer science to make a difference in people's lives. As teenage girls who have a passion for fashion, we want to help provide other men and women with our own fashion tips, a convenient source of the latest trends, and a safe, supportive atmosphere where people have the ability to share their own style of expression.
