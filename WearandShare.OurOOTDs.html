TRENDING STYLES WE ♡

Sometimes it can be a little hard keeping up with the latest trends. Here are our personal favorites and how we fit it into our own style.

Fanny Packs
 




Whether you are wearing it on your shoulder or on your waist, a fanny pack is a great way to spice up any outfit. Not only is it super cute, but it's a great way to store your everyday items that may be too small to carry in a purse or backpack.






Prints
 




Don't be boring, throw on a cool print! Adding a top with some kind of print is a great way to make your outfit fun. Polka dots can make you seem both playful and chic.






Cardigans
 




A cozy look is always nice. Cardigans are pretty versatile since they're perfect for staying warm in the winter and they add the perfect texture to your outfit during the summer. Cardigans can fit in any outfit really.






Layering
 





Layering is perfect when you want to be casual but still want to make it seem like you put a lot of thought in your outfit. It's the perfect way to dress up any t-shirt you have laying around!
